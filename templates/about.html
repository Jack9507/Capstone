About Us

E-Learning is the process of using technology to assist learning and tutoring. Enhancement in how the Internet works has allowed websites to deliver a visual and immersive learning experience on a computer or mobile device, perfect for stimulating a student and encouraging learning.

Explore more if you want to learn more.This website is designed to help students learn and grow in the best way possible.

One of the most important areas we can develop as professionals is competence in accessing and sharing knowledge.

  • Courses available for Computer Science Students.
  • Login and create your account to keep a track of your learning.
  • E-Learning is changing. And, we will see new models, new technologies and designs emerge. So, let’s drop the “E” – or at least give it a new and wider definition.
